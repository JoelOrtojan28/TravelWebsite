my top 6 provinces

Top 1

Bohol Province

Bohol is an island province located in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines. It is known for its natural beauty, unique attractions, and rich cultural heritage.

Top 2

Aurora Province

Aurora is a province located on the eastern coast of Luzon Island in the Philippines. It is known for its stunning natural landscapes, including lush forests, beautiful waterfalls, pristine beaches, and the mighty Sierra Madre mountain range.

Top 3

Cebu Province

Cebu is a province in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines. It is a vibrant and dynamic destination known for its rich history, stunning beaches, vibrant festivals, and diverse cultural attractions.

Top 4

Benguet Province

Benguet is a province located in the Cordillera Administrative Region of the Philippines. Known as the "Salad Bowl of the Philippines," Benguet is celebrated for its cool climate, breathtaking mountain landscapes, and extensive agricultural production.

Top 5

Mountain Province

Mountain Province is a landlocked province located in the Cordillera Administrative Region of the Philippines. It is renowned for its breathtaking mountain landscapes, rich indigenous culture, and ancient rice terraces.

Top 6

Bukidnon Province

Bukidnon Province is a landlocked province located in the Northern Mindanao region of the Philippines. It is known as the "Food Basket of Mindanao" due to its vast agricultural lands and abundant produce.
